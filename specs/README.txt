These are a couple of databases made publicly available by the unicode consortium 
and are paramount to implementing a correct unicode library.

- Encoder Mappings.
 * http://unicode.org/Public/MAPPINGS/

- Unicode mapping database.
 * ftp://ftp.unicode.org/Public/UNIDATA/UnicodeData.txt
 * info: http://www.unicode.org/faq/casemap_charprop.html
